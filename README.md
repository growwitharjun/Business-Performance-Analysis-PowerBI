# Business Performance & Cost Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project simulates a real-world business scenario where leadership requires visibility into revenue, costs, and profitability to support data-driven decisions.

The dashboard is designed using an executive-first approach, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and actionable insights.

## Business Questions Addressed
- How is overall revenue and profit performing?
- Which regions contribute the most to revenue?
- How do fixed and variable costs change over time?
- Is profit growth aligned with revenue growth?

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI
- DAX Measures
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)
- Time Intelligence
- Business Analysis

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
- Revenue, Cost, Profit, and Profit Margin KPIs
- Monthly revenue trend
- Revenue comparison by region

### 2. Cost & Profit Analysis
- Fixed vs Variable cost structure by month
- Profit trends over time
- Interactive filters for region and product category

## Key Insights
- Revenue growth does not always translate to higher profit due to rising variable costs.
- Certain regions drive higher revenue but show weaker profitability.
- Cost structure analysis helps identify operational efficiency challenges.

## Outcome
This project demonstrates how a Business Analyst translates raw data into insights that support strategic decision-making.
